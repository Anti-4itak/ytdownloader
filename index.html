About YouTubeDownloader

The App can be accessed via the share button in the YouTube App, while watching a video. Then choose ‘YouTube Downloader’ and you will have further usage info. You can also share any YouTube plain text link via any other App: i.e. your browser, or sharing a link via your mail App.

You’ll see all the video codecs and qualities available for the video you’re on.

You can:

  • Press an item to download the video locally (on the device) or to send the download via SSH to a remote PC. Destination machine requirements: Bash and Wget. Additional SSH info using the App;
  • Long-press an item and choose between copy to clipboard or share link as text (i.e. send to another app). Note: such a link (differently from when you send it via SSH) can be only used from the same device where it has been created OR from the same LAN, if you share it to another PC.

Audio extraction feature will be available from next version (v2.0). To extract or convert the audio to mp3 from a downloaded video:

  • Enable the audio preference and download FFmpeg
  • Choose the extraction type
  • 'extract only' will conserve the audio stream from the video as is (WebM files contain an 'Ogg Vorbis' audio; MP4, 3GPP and large/medium FLV video hold an 'AAC' audio stream; FLV small holds an 'mp3')
  • 'extract and encode to mp3' will convert the extracted audio in an mp3, encoding it with the selected bitrate
  • From now on, a checkbox will appear in the download confirmation box visible when you click an item in the YTD's video list. When checked, the audio extraction job will start upon video download completion (to hide the checkbox, disable the audio extraction option)
  • Choose from settings if you want to remove the video file after a seccessful audio extraction
